Background

With the travel sector struggling due to the credit crunch,Expedia made the decision to launch an ambitious wiral project,codenamed "Project Dawnstar".

The objective of Project Dawnstar to drive eyeballs to the Expedia.com(and lesser extent co.uk)website and attract inb ound links which could then pushed to enhance Google Rankings,while showing it was still possible to have fun during otherwise gloomy times.

Further,with 2 other agencies and working with Expedia's internal team helped plan their 'Flights to Mars for $99'April fool for launch on April 1st.

Aside from helping in the palnning and execution of the viral,Further would help make the campaign go viral using the networks Digg.com,Reddit.com and StumbleUpon,as well as cross-promoting a faux-advert produced by Expedia in house.

Results

  • Mian Page (http://www.expedia.com/daily/mars/about-mars/default.asp)went into popular category on Digg.com
